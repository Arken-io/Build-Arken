1. What we use, and why

We keep this deliberately small. Here's everything BuildArken stores in your browser:

NameTypePurposeDuration
Supabase session cookieCookieKeeps you signed in, shared across BuildArken's tools (Compare, Chat, Code Check)30 days
arken_langLocal storageRemembers your chosen languageUntil cleared
arken_themeLocal storageRemembers Dark/Light preferenceUntil cleared
Google Analytics (_ga, etc.)CookieAggregate, anonymized traffic statisticsUp to 2 years

2. Strictly necessary

The session cookie is required for sign-in to work — without it, you'd be logged out on every page. Local storage for language/theme is similarly functional, not tracking.

3. Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand overall traffic and usage patterns. This data is aggregated and not tied back to your BuildArken profile.

4. Controlling cookies

You can clear cookies and local storage from your browser settings at any time — this will sign you out and reset your language/theme preference back to default. Most browsers also let you block third-party cookies (like Google Analytics) without affecting sign-in.

5. Changes

If what we store changes, we'll update this page and the date above.
